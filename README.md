# 📊 Financial Performance Analysis (Power BI)

An end-to-end financial performance dashboard built in Power BI, focused on **KPI monitoring, cost structure analysis, and management-level insights** — comparing Actual results against Previous Year and Budget across YTD, quarterly, and monthly views.

`Power BI` · `DAX` ·  `Power Query` · `Financial KPI Modeling`

---

## 🎯 Business Goal

Management needs to quickly see where financial performance is deviating from plan — and why — without digging through raw ledger exports. This dashboard turns raw financial results (Revenue, COGS, Variable Costs, OPEX) into decision-ready KPIs (Gross Margin, Contribution Margin, EBITDA) compared against Previous Year and Budget, so leadership can spot underperforming periods and cost drivers at a glance.

## 📊 Key Analytical Areas

| Area | Focus |
|---|---|
| **KPI Analysis** | Gross Profit Margin, Contribution Margin, EBITDA, cost-to-revenue ratios (COGS, Variable Costs, OPEX) |
| **Cost Structure** | Variable costs (Warehouse, Distribution), OPEX (Google vs. Meta marketing spend), cost rigidity vs. revenue decline |
| **Trend Analysis** | Quarterly comparisons, monthly deep-dives, identifying critical periods (e.g. April) |

Data is sliceable by **market** (e.g. DE) and time granularity (YTD / Quarterly / Monthly), always compared against **PY** and **Budget**.

## ✅ Key Findings

- Overall H1 performance is **negative vs. both PY and Budget**
- Slight improvement only in Gross Profit Margin YTD and COGS Ratio YTD
- Other profitability metrics deteriorated — driven by revenue decline combined with insufficient cost reduction in Variable Costs and OPEX
- Contribution Margin and EBITDA show significant pressure on operational profitability
